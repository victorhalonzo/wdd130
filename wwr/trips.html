Quijos, Napo, Ecuador

In the Quijos canton we can find the town of the same name where rafting takes place, which over time has become an adventure activity that is enjoyed by young and old alike. From the by pass sector, between the road to El Chaco and Quito, once located in the river, the rafting safety talk is given and the descent begins until reaching Borja through river rapids for a period of 4 hours. You rest to serve lunch and after this you continue with the adventure until you reach the mouth of the Oyacachi River, where the tour ends.
