Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help customers navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and/or the guides they need to safely head down the river.

Audience

  • Who are the target customers? Amateur people and tourists.
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Information about the trips, reservations and pricing.
  • How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Laptops and movile devices.
