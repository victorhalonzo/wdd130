The purpose of this website is to help customers navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and/or the guides they need to safely head down the river.

We offer information about trips, prices, reservations and some testimonials from our clients. If you come rafting on your birthday we will give you 50% of the cost of the rafting to celebrate. We will help you organize a special party, mixing the excitement of the river with the pleasure of enjoying a unique natural setting in the Alps at the foot of Mount Blanc. At our base you will always find a relaxing free space for barbecues, parties or events.
